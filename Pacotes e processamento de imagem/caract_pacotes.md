## Formato de pacotes python:

Estrutura de um pacote simples:

    project_name/
        README.md
        setup.py
        requirements.txt
        package_name/
            __init__.py
            file_name.py
            file_name.py


Estrutura de um pacote com vários módulos:

    project_name/
        README.md
        setup.py
        requirements.txt
        package_name/
            __init__.py
            module1_name/
                __init__.py
                file_name.py
                file_name.py
            module2_name/
                __init__.py
                file_name.py
                file_name.py
